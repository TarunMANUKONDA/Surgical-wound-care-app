## Required Images and Names:

### 1. early-stage.jpg
- **Your image**: The sutured wound with the measuring ruler
- **Description**: Shows fresh surgical wound with sutures and measurement scale
- **Size**: Any resolution (will be resized automatically)

### 2. mid-stage.jpg
- **Your image**: The close-up of sutured wound with visible stitches  
- **Description**: Shows wound with multiple sutures, good closure, minimal inflammation
- **Size**: Any resolution (will be resized automatically)

### 3. progression.jpg
- **Your image**: The three-panel progression image
- **Description**: Three side-by-side views showing healing progression over time
- **Size**: Any resolution (will be resized automatically)

## How to Add Images:

1. Open File Explorer
2. Navigate to: `C:\Users\MANISH\Downloads\design-wound-care-app-screens\public\images\healing-stages\`
3. Copy your 3 uploaded images into this folder
4. Rename them to match the names above:
   - early-stage.jpg
   - mid-stage.jpg
   - progression.jpg

5. The app will automatically display them on the Healing Levels screen!
